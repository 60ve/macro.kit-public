# macro.kit

this is a non-jailbreak touch macro engine and autoclicker for ios. it uses private uikit and iohid apis to inject hardware lvl touch events directly into the app, so you don't need a jailbreak for it to work.

### what it is
- **macros**: record your own gestures ( swipes, taps, multi finger moves ) and play them back at up to 100x speed.
- **autoclicker**: set multiple targets on your screen and cycle through them with custom clicls.
- **adaptive ui**: the floating menu glides smoothly and stays out of your way by picking the emptiest spot on the screen.

### how to use
1. **record**: hit the "start recording" button, do your gestures, then hit stop. your macro is saved as macro_1, macro_2, etc.
2. **play**: select your macro from the dropdown, set your speed, and hit play. it will automatically lift all touches when finished.
3. **loop**: toggle the loop button if you want it to repeat forever.
4. **autoclick**: switch to autoclick mode, tap "start autoclicker," tap all the spots on your screen you want to click, then hit "done."
